# Privacy Policy for Fitness Track

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Fitness Track is provided by Trailmark Works.

## Information We Collect

Fitness Track stores your fitness tracking information locally on your device. The app does not require an account, does not collect personal information on a server, and does not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Health and Fitness Data

If Fitness Track stores or displays health or fitness-related information, that information is used only to provide the app’s features.

If Fitness Track integrates with Apple Health, Fitness Track may request permission to read or write selected health or workout data. Any Apple Health data is used only to provide app features you choose to use and is not sold or shared with third parties.

## Analytics, Cloud Sync, and Accounts

Fitness Track does not use account registration, advertising, or cloud sync.
